About Academic

Teaching Methodology

"Education is the kindling of a flame not the filling of a vessel”.
We, at Green Park, not only subscribe to the conventional method of teaching through 'chalk and talk' method, but also adopt the latest technology in teaching. Smart classes and hands-on - training foster crystallized and clear understanding of all the contents taught.

Teachers at Green Park

The Teachers at Green Park are of high profile with unmatched dedication. They gain expertise in making teaching a pleasurable adventure through the impact of their Directors, who are innovative, creative and committed to serve children in multitudes. These teachers inculcate a desire in the young minds to learn and excel in their respective subjects. Service oriented staff offer valuable inputs to obtain remarkable achievements in every child.
Headed by the motivating Directors, the inspired team of adequately qualified and experienced teaching fraternity gain knowledge and become erudite. These powerful teachers pass on their spirit of excellence to the entire student community and make Green Park soar newer heights of excellence and leave a legacy behind.

Curriculum

The Syllabus and course of study are designed as per the Central Board of Secondary Education,New Delhi and NCERT.
We follow separate homework schedule for each class to balance the home work.
Our school strictly implement “no homework policy” for classes I and II.

Co-curricular activities and Extra Curricular activities

• Special coaching is offered in Music, Drawing, Painting, Essay writing, Elocution and Quiz.
• NSS Girls unit is functioning so as to create social awareness among students.
• Cubs and Bulbuls function for primary classes.
• Our boys and girls consistently win Heerak Pankh & Chaturth Charan awards.
• Scouts and Guides are functioning for boys and girls studying from VI std to X std.
• Our children win Rajya Puraskar awards every year. .
